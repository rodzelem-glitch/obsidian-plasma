# TekTrakker AI Training Data - Customer Role

This document details the features and capabilities available to the **Customer** user role via the self-service portal.

## 1. Customer Role Overview
*   **Primary Dashboard**: `/portal`
*   **Key Objective**: To provide homeowners with a secure, centralized place to manage their service history, book new appointments, handle billing, and communicate with their service provider.

---

## 2. Feature Deep Dive: Customer Portal Capabilities

### 2.1. Dashboard Overview
*   **Purpose**: The main landing page for the customer, providing a summary of key information.
*   **Key Information**:
    *   Shows the currently assigned technician if a job is in progress.
    *   Displays upcoming appointments.
    *   Highlights any unpaid invoices that require action.
    *   Shows the status of their Membership Plan.

### 2.2. Core Features
*   **Book New Service**:
    *   **How-To**: A prominent "Book Service" button allows customers to open a modal and request a new appointment. They can specify the type of service needed (e.g., Repair, Maintenance), a desired date, and add notes.
*   **View Service History**:
    *   **Purpose**: Provides a complete, searchable history of all past jobs.
    *   **Capabilities**:
        *   Customers can see a list of all completed jobs.
        *   They can click on a past job to view a detailed **Service Report**, which includes:
            *   A list of tasks performed.
            *   Technician's notes.
            *   Any photos taken during the job.
            *   A link to the final invoice/receipt.
*   **Manage Invoices & Billing**:
    *   **Purpose**: A dedicated section to handle all financial matters.
    *   **Capabilities**:
        *   View a list of all unpaid invoices with their due amounts.
        *   Click a "PAY NOW" button to open a secure payment page for a specific invoice.
        *   View a history of all past paid invoices.
*   **Review & Approve Proposals**:
    *   **Purpose**: Allows customers to act on quotes sent by technicians.
    *   **Capabilities**:
        *   View a list of pending proposals.
        *   Open a detailed view of any proposal to see the line items and options.
        *   If the proposal is ready for acceptance, a button will appear allowing them to **electronically sign** and approve the work directly in the portal.
*   **Manage Membership Plans**:
    *   **Purpose**: To manage their service agreement with the company.
    *   **Capabilities**:
        *   View their current membership status (e.g., "Gold Guardian Member").
        *   See key details like their next renewal date and estimated savings.
        *   If they are not a member, they can browse available plans, view the benefits of each, and sign up directly through the portal using PayPal for recurring billing.
*   **Multi-Provider Support**:
    *   **Capability**: If a homeowner uses multiple service companies that all use TekTrakker, they can link their accounts. A dropdown menu appears in the header, allowing them to switch between the portals for each provider seamlessly.
*   **Profile Management**:
    *   Customers can update their basic contact information, including their name, phone number, and primary address.
